1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Family Drug Cabinet app ("App") handles your data when you use our medication tracking, reminder, and management features.

2. Data We Process

2.1 Data You Enter (May Include Sensitive Health Data)

  • Family/member profiles: Names, year of birth (if you choose to enter)
  • Health information: Allergies, health conditions (liver/kidney, pregnancy), medical notes
  • Medication data: Medicine names, dosages, schedules, usage history
  • Images: Photos from scanning medicine labels or prescriptions (if you use this feature)

2.2 Technical Data

  • Advertising: Google AdMob may collect Advertising ID and device information for ad delivery
  • Crash reports: We do NOT collect crash reports or analytics off-device

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide medication tracking, reminders, and safety alerts
  • Display reference information about medications you store
  • Deliver non-personalized advertisements via AdMob

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data remains on your device until:

  • You delete individual items within the app
  • You use "Delete All Data" in Settings
  • You uninstall the app (subject to OS backup retention)

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • View and edit all data directly in the app
  • Delete any or all data at any time
  • Disable notifications in Settings
  • Withdraw consent (Reset to welcome screen via Settings)

7. Children's Privacy

This app is NOT directed at children for unsupervised use. If you enter data for children/dependents, you confirm you have legal authority to do so and take responsibility for data accuracy.

8. Security

We apply reasonable measures to protect data on your device. We recommend:

  • Using device lock screen protection
  • Not sharing your device with unauthorized persons
  • Not publicly posting sensitive health information

We may update this Policy. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.
