FAMILY DRUG CABINET

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 12, 2024

Our Commitment: Family Drug Cabinet ("App") always respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you provide to us.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Information You Provide Directly

  • Medicine Information: Medicine names, dosages, expiration dates, usage notes
  • Family Member Information: Names, ages, allergy information, health conditions (if you choose to enter)
  • Medication Schedules: Reminder times, medication usage history

1.2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version (for debugging purposes)
  • Usage Data: Anonymous statistics about how you use the app

1.3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No precise location data – We do not access GPS or location services
  • No personal identifiers – We do not collect email, phone number, or government IDs
  • No health records from external sources – We do not connect to hospitals or health systems
  • No biometric data – We do not collect fingerprints, face data, or voice recordings

2. Account Requirements

No account required: The App does not require the creation of user accounts. All features work without login or registration. Your data remains entirely on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide the medicine cabinet management features of the app
  • Send medication reminders and expiration alerts
  • Check drug interactions and provide safety warnings
  • Improve user experience and app performance
  • Display relevant advertisements (if you use the free version)

4. Data Storage

Local Data Storage: All medicine information, family member data, and usage history are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your personal data on our servers.

  • Data is stored in a secure database on your device
  • You have complete control over your data
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all local data
  • We recommend regular backups as data cannot be recovered after uninstallation

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Advertising Partners (Google AdMob): Receive anonymous data to display relevant ads
  • Legal Requirements: When required by competent authorities under applicable law

6. Third-Party Services

6.1. Google AdMob (Advertising)

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (Advertising ID)
  • IP address (for general location-based ad targeting)
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, impressions)

You can disable personalized ads in your device settings or upgrade to Premium to remove ads entirely.

6.2. No Other Third-Party SDKs

Apart from Google AdMob for advertising and Google Play Billing for Premium purchases, no other third-party SDKs are integrated into this app that collect user data.

7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:

  • Data is stored in Android's encrypted database
  • The app does not require login or user accounts
  • No network connection is required for core features
  • All sensitive health data remains on your device only

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app
  • Edit: Update or modify information at any time
  • Delete: Remove individual items or uninstall the app to delete all data
  • Export: Back up your data (if feature is available)
  • Opt-out: Disable personalized ads via device settings

9. Children's Privacy

The app is not designed to collect information from children under 13 years of age. Family member information (including children) is only entered and managed by responsible adults. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

10. International Users

This app is available globally. By using the app, you consent to the processing of your information as described in this policy. For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process data based on legitimate interests and your consent where applicable.

11. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated through the app or appropriate channels. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the current version.
